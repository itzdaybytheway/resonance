About

Our Mission

Resonance was created by two high school students, hoping to create a tutoring organization that helps people get better at their instrument. Since then, we have put in hundreds of hours tutoring students and building this organization.

Our Standards

Generally, we tutor beginning students that have just begun their instrument to intermediate students that have played for a few years. We try to remain in the age range from 6 to 14, so we can offer adequate help to the student. Usually, we prefer students that are already enrolled in a school band, but we can take students that are not. Our tutoring curriculum consists of tone production, fundamentals, basic music theory, and much more.
